Homemade BBQ sauce

How to Make a homemade BBQ sauce

Here's what you'll need to make the homemade BBQ sauce of your dreams:

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, vinegar, water, and Worcestershire sauce in a blender. Season with mustard, paprika, salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce. Blend until smooth.
  3. Enjoy!
